About Me

Hi! My name is Evan Grau. If you're here, that means you wanted a more detailed explanation of who I am. Let's get started.

I was born in Bluffton, Indiana on September 22, 2002. I lived there until I was about 13 years old, when my family and I moved to Chandler, Arizona because my dad got a job at Arizona State University. I lived there until I was about 16 years old when we moved to Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. My family has lived there since we moved in 2019. The last half of my junior year of high school was when COVID first hit the US and affected how public schools were functioning. My entire senior year of high school was affected by COVID in some fashion, but that didn't stop me from becoming Valedictorian of my class. After graduating from high school, I started applying to colleges, but ACU was the one that really caught my eye. I took a tour of the campus and that's what sold me on attending ACU.
